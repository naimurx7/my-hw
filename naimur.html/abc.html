COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nandi really better than Aashirvaad?

Nandi is the #1 Best Seller on Blinkit in Atta with a 4.5-star rating across 25,000+ reviews, ahead of national brands in our home markets. We use 100% Sharbati wheat from MP and grind it daily in our FSSC 22000 certified facility.

Why is a UP-based brand #1 on Blinkit?

Because we're local. Our mill is in Prayagraj, our wheat comes from MP, and we deliver fresh every day. No long supply chains, no stale stock.

Where can I buy Nandi products?

Available on Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, BigBasket, and 25,000+ retail stores across UP.

What is the shelf life of Nandi products?

Our atta has a shelf life of 6 months from the date of manufacture when stored in a cool, dry place.

Are Nandi products 100% vegetarian?

Yes, all Nandi products are 100% vegetarian with no animal-derived ingredients.

How can I become a Nandi distributor?

Visit our Become a Distributor page or contact us at our Prayagraj office for distribution opportunities.

Can I order Nandi products in bulk?

Yes! We supply in bulk to restaurants, caterers, and institutions. Contact us for bulk pricing.

What certifications does Nandi Ecavo have?

We are FSSC 22000 certified, FSSAI licensed, and ISO compliant. All products are lab-tested before dispatch.
